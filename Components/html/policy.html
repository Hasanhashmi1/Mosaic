Shipping Policy

Learn about our delivery processes, timelines, and shipping options

Shipping Overview

At Mosaic, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and efficiently. Our shipping policy outlines the different options available, expected delivery times, and how we handle various shipping scenarios.

Nationwide Coverage

We ship to all major cities and towns across India through our trusted logistics partners.

Careful Packaging

All items are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit.

Shipping Options & Delivery Times

We offer multiple shipping options to suit your needs. Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on location and other factors.

Service Delivery Time Price Features
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days ₹49 Tracking available
Express Shipping 2-3 business days ₹149 Priority handling, tracking
Same-Day Delivery Within 24 hours* ₹299 Available in select metros, order by 12PM
Free Shipping 7-10 business days FREE On orders over ₹2000

*Same-day delivery available in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and Hyderabad only for orders placed before 12PM on business days.

Order Processing

Understanding our order processing timeline helps set accurate delivery expectations.

Processing Time

Most orders are processed within 24-48 hours of being placed. Orders placed after 3PM or on weekends will be processed the next business day.

Business Days

Our business days are Monday through Saturday, excluding public holidays. Orders are not processed or shipped on Sundays or holidays.

Order Notifications

You'll receive email notifications at each stage: order confirmation, shipping confirmation, and delivery updates.

International Shipping

We currently offer limited international shipping to select countries.

Available Destinations

USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Singapore. Contact us for shipping to other countries.

Customs & Duties

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country. These charges are not included in the order total.

Delivery Times

International orders typically take 10-15 business days for delivery, depending on destination and customs processing.

Order Tracking & Support

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package using our Order Status page or directly with the carrier.

Shipping Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact our customer support team immediately. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the problem.

Address Changes

Address changes can only be made if the order hasn't been processed for shipping. Contact us immediately if you need to change your shipping address.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

Damaged Items

If your order arrives damaged, please take photos of the packaging and product, and contact us within 48 hours of delivery for assistance.

Lost Packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received it, contact us immediately. We'll initiate an investigation with the carrier.

Return Shipping

For eligible returns, we provide prepaid return labels. Please see our Return Policy for complete details.

Need Help With Shipping?

Our customer support team is available to answer any shipping-related questions.
