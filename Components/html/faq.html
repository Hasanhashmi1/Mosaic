Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gift cards

General Questions

What types of gift cards do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of gift cards including clothing, accessories, shoes, and multi-brand cards that can be used across our entire collection. Our cards come in different denominations to suit every budget.

How do I purchase a gift card?

Purchasing a gift card is simple! Just browse our gift card section, select the card you want, choose the amount, and add it to your cart. You can pay using any major credit/debit card or digital payment methods.

Can I use multiple gift cards for one purchase?

Yes, you can combine up to 3 gift cards for a single purchase. During checkout, you'll have the option to apply multiple gift card codes to your order.

Redemption & Usage

How do I redeem my gift card?

To redeem your gift card, simply enter the unique code found on your card during checkout. The amount will be automatically deducted from your total. You can view your balance at any time by entering the code on our gift card balance page.

Can I check my gift card balance?

Absolutely! Visit our gift card balance page and enter your card's unique code to see the remaining balance. You can also see the balance during checkout before completing your purchase.

Do gift cards expire?

Our gift cards never expire! The full value remains available until it's completely used. However, please note that promotional gift cards may have different terms.

Troubleshooting

What should I do if my gift card isn't working?

First, double-check that you've entered the code correctly. If it still doesn't work, please contact our customer support team with your gift card details and they'll help resolve the issue promptly.

Can I get a refund for my gift card?

Gift cards are non-refundable except as required by law. However, if you have any issues, please contact our support team and we'll do our best to assist you.

What happens if my order total is less than the gift card amount?

The remaining balance will stay on your gift card for future use. You can check the remaining balance anytime on our website.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is happy to help with any other questions you may have.
