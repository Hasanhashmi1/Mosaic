Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2024. Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Mosaic ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website.

Key Point: By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy, unless you have disabled them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to:

  • Make websites work more efficiently
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide personalized content
  • Analyze website traffic

Cookies may be either "persistent" (remain on your device until deleted) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies for different purposes:

Type Purpose Examples
Essential Necessary for website functionality Login sessions, shopping cart
Performance Help improve our website Analytics, error tracking
Functional Remember preferences Language settings, font size
Targeting Personalize content/ads Social media integrations

Third-Party Cookies

We also allow certain third parties to place cookies on our website for:

  • Analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising (Facebook Pixel)
  • Social media integrations
  • Payment processing

These third parties have their own privacy policies and may use information about your visits to our and other websites to provide relevant advertisements.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Opt-out tools: For third-party cookies, visit AboutAds or Your Online Choices
  • Our preference center: Use the cookie settings button in our footer

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

  • Web beacons: Tiny graphics with unique identifiers
  • Local storage: Stores data in your browser
  • Device fingerprinting: Identifies devices based on characteristics

These technologies help us understand user behavior and improve our services.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Requesting renewed consent for cookies
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
