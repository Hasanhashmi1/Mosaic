Returns & Exchange Policy

Hassle-free returns and exchanges for your peace of mind

Returns Overview

At Mosaic, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer easy returns and exchanges within our policy period.

Note: Gift cards are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged unless required by law.

Return Window

You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Some items may have different return timeframes (see below).

Condition Requirements

Items must be unused, in original condition with all tags attached, and in original packaging. We reserve the right to refuse returns that don't meet these conditions.

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your account and go to "Order History" to start the return process. You'll need your order number and email address.

Print Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label. Print it and attach it securely to your package.

Package Your Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible. Include all original tags, accessories, and free gifts.

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping center. You'll receive a confirmation email when we receive your return.

Processing Time

Please allow 5-7 business days for your return to be processed after we receive it. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Exchange Process

Want a different size, color, or style? Here's how exchanges work:

Request an Exchange

Start the exchange process through your account within 30 days of delivery. Select the item you want instead.

Ship Your Original Item

Use the provided return label to send back your original purchase. The item must meet our return conditions.

Receive Your New Item

Once we receive your return, we'll ship your exchange item. You'll be notified via email with tracking information.

Important Notes

Exchanges are subject to availability. If your requested item is out of stock, we'll contact you to discuss alternatives or issue a refund.

Refund Information

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement after processing.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error. Return shipping is free for domestic returns.

Sale Items

Sale items are eligible for exchange or store credit only, unless defective or incorrect.

Exceptions & Special Cases

Final Sale Items

Clearance items, intimate apparel, and personalized items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for expedited resolution.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

Holiday Return Policy

For purchases made between November 1st and December 31st, we extend our return window until January 31st of the following year.

Gift Returns

If you received an item as a gift, you can exchange it for store credit with proof of purchase. Contact our customer service for assistance.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer support team is happy to assist with any return or exchange questions.
