How to Measure Yourself

For accurate measurements, use a soft measuring tape and follow these guidelines:

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline (above your belly button)

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips

Pro Tip: Wear form-fitting clothing when measuring and don't pull the tape too tight.
